The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Learn how to make the best chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • 3/4 cup of white sugar
  • 3/4 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  3. Beat butter, white and brown sugar together with an electric mixer until smooth.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  5. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter alon with salt.
  6. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
  8. Bake in preheated oven until the edges are nicely browned, approximately 10 minutes
  9. After removing from the oven put cookies on a wire rack to cool.
  10. Enjoy!
